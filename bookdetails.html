About the Book

In "The Silent Echo," Sarah J. Matthews weaves a spellbinding tale of mystery and suspense. When detective Anna Clarke stumbles upon an ancient artifact in the depths of a forgotten cave, she unknowingly sets in motion a chain of events that will challenge everything she knows about reality. As the boundaries between past and present blur, Anna must race against time to decipher cryptic messages and prevent a catastrophe that threatens to reshape humanity's future.
